# Changelog

All notable changes to Heart of Life Additions will be documented here.

---

## [1.0.1]

### Added
- **Heart Scaling System** Tool speed and sword damage now scale with your current heart count
    - 1–9 hearts: 90% tool speed and sword damage (slight debuff)
    - 10 hearts: Base stats
    - 11–15 hearts: +10% tool speed and sword damage
    - 16–20 hearts: +20% tool speed and sword damage

- **Heart Armor Set Bonus** Wearing the full set of heart armor grants passive effects
    - 1–9 hearts: No set bonus
    - 10–15 hearts: Regen II (3s) + Absorption I (10s) on a 25s cooldown
    - 16–20 hearts: Regen II (3s) + Absorption II (10s) on a 20s cooldown + permanent Speed I

- **Fireproof** All heart tools, weapons, and armor are now immune to fire and lava damage

---

## [1.0.0]

### Added
- **Heart Upgrade Smithing Template** Epic rarity smithing template used to upgrade diamond gear into heart gear
    - Crafted using diamonds, a netherite upgrade template, and a Heart of Life
    - Can be placed in the smithing template slot of a smithing table
    - Custom tooltip showing "Applies to: Diamond Armor & Tools" and "Ingredients: Heart of Life"

- **Heart Themed Tools and Weapons** Full set of heart-infused tools with netherite-tier stats
    - Heart Sword, Heart Pickaxe, Heart Axe, Heart Shovel, Heart Hoe

- **Heart Armor** Full set of heart-themed armor with netherite-tier stats
    - Heart Helmet, Heart Chestplate, Heart Leggings, Heart Boots
    - Custom worn armor textures

- **Creative Tab** All heart items added to the Heart of Life creative tab

- **Smithing Tag** Heart Upgrade Template correctly slots into the smithing template slot

- **Language Support** Full en_us translations for all items, tooltips, and creative tab

- **Recipes**
    - Smithing transform recipes for all 9 gear pieces (diamond to heart)
    - Crafting recipe for the Heart Upgrade Smithing Template
